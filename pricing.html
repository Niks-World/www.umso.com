    Do I have to pay extra for more users?

    No, we don't do seat-based pricing. Invite your entire team to collaborate on your site!

    When and how can I cancel?

    You can cancel at any time in your account settings. There are no long-term contracts and you do not have to call us to cancel.

    How can I pay?

    We accept major credit cards. European customers can pay in Euro and through additional providers including Bancontact, EPS, giropay, iDEAL, Sofort, SEPA.

    Are there any visitor or pageview limits?

    No. We don't want you to worry about getting too popular. That means you can have as many site visitors and page views as you like.

    Do I need a Credit Card for free sites?

    No. You only need to add a credit card to pay for Pro sites. Free sites just require a Free account.

    Is there a free trial?

    No. We don't have trials. You can simply create a free site, which has all features except for custom domains and site export.
